# Restoloop

WhatsApp-first customer retention platform for Indian restaurants. Restaurants onboard customers via QR code and WhatsApp DM, then automatically send coupon reminders to bring them back.

## Language

**Customer**:
A person who has opted in to receive coupons from a restaurant via WhatsApp or the web intake form.
_Avoid_: Guest (UI-only alias), Diner (too generic)
